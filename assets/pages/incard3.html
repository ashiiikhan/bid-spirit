autumn Oil on canvas 50x40 signed. One of the paintings from the last series that Baruch Alron painted. Baruch Alron, Israeli, born in Romania, 1934-2006. Baruch Elron was born in Bucharest, Romania, in 1934. He studied art in Bucharest. Beside his practice in art, he made a living by working in illustration and drawing cartoons. In 1963 immigrated to Israel. Initially engaged in graphic design. He then served as an officer in the training department of the Israel Police and as an art teacher. In the 80's of the 20th century he was one of the members of the surrealist "Meshosha" group. In 1988 he was elected to serve as the national chairman of the Society of Painters and Sculptors. Elron's main work was in the medium of painting. His works bore a figurative-surrealistic character and included connections to Jewish and Western mysticism. Studies Master's degree, Academy of Art, Bucharest, Romania
