LOT 85:

REDUCED: Bone Fertility Statue - STC
A unique and captivating art piece crafted from a large rib bone.
Carved to depict a mother figure cradling twins in her arms, while the infants are shown suckling at her breasts. The intricate
details showcase the bond between mother and
children, emphasizing fertility and the nurturing aspects of motherhood.
The use of a rib bone adds an intriguing and symbolic element to the sculpture, connecting it to the concept of life and creation.
The artist likely aimed to convey a sense of fertility, abundance, and the cycle of life through this sculpture.
The choice of twins could also signify the idea of duality, balance,
and the blessings of multiple births in certain cultural contexts.
This fertility statue serves as a powerful representation of life,
motherhood, and the enduring cycle of creation. The combination of the rib bone material and the depiction of twins
